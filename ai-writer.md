## What is AI Writer?

Arc AI Writer is a one-tap writing assistant that lives in the floating sidebar. It understands the text on your screen and helps you:

- **Rewrite** any text in a different tone or style
- **Fix grammar** and spelling mistakes instantly
- **Generate replies** to messages, emails, and comments
- **Translate** text between languages while preserving meaning
- **Create posts** for social media, captions, or short-form content

No need to switch apps or copy-paste into another tool. Just tap the Arc icon, choose a writing mode, and get a ready-to-use result.
