## 4. Permissions and Data Access

Arc requires specific Android permissions to function. We are transparent about what we request and why:

| Permission | Purpose | When Active |
| :--------- | :------ | :---------- |
| **Accessibility Service** | Read on-screen text and capture screenshots (with consent) | Only when you explicitly tap a sidebar action (AI Summary, AI Read, Save Content, Custom Action, or Chat). **Never** runs in the background or monitors your screen automatically. |
| **Display Over Other Apps (Overlay)** | Show the floating sidebar on top of other applications | When the sidebar service is running (you can close it anytime) |
| **Internet / Network State** | Communicate with AI services and our backend | When using AI features or Google Sign-In |
