<!-- FAQ -->
<div class="section-header">
  <h2>FAQ</h2>
  <p>Everything you need to know about Arc AI.</p>
</div>

<details><summary>Do I need to create an account?</summary><p>No! Use Arc as a guest. Google Sign-In is optional — only needed for cloud backup and community features.</p></details>
<details><summary>Does Arc monitor my screen in the background?</summary><p>No. Arc only accesses screen content when you explicitly tap a sidebar button. Never passively.</p></details>
<details><summary>Are screenshots automatically captured?</summary><p>No. Screenshots require explicit permission and are only taken when you initiate an action that needs them.</p></details>
<details><summary>Which apps work with Arc?</summary><p>Any app — browsers, social media, email, documents. Arc auto-disables in ~400 sensitive apps (banking, crypto, password managers) by default.</p></details>
<details><summary>Is my data private?</summary><p>Yes. Screen content is processed for AI but never stored on our servers. Saved content stays on your device unless you choose Google Drive backup. See <a href="privacy.html">Privacy Policy</a>.</p></details>
<details><summary>What are Custom Actions?</summary><p>Create your own AI commands. Write a prompt like "Translate to Spanish" and save it to your sidebar for one-tap access.</p></details>
<details><summary>What are Community Actions?</summary><p>AI actions shared by other users. Browse, search, and add them to your sidebar — or publish your own.</p></details>
<details><summary>How does Chat with AI work?</summary><p>Chat about content on your screen. Ask questions, get clarifications, attach screenshots for visual context.</p></details>
<details><summary>What is AI Writer?</summary><p>Four modes: Rewrite (change tone), Fix Grammar, Reply (contextual responses), and Create Post (social media). Plus My Info Vault for personalization.</p></details>
<details><summary>How do Flashcards work?</summary><p>Generate study flashcards from any screen content. Preview before saving, build a library, review with spaced repetition.</p></details>
<details><summary>What is AI Call Insights?</summary><p>Analyze call recordings with Manual Upload or Auto Analysis. Get transcriptions, summaries, action items, and insights.</p></details>
<details><summary>What is Smart Extract?</summary><p>Extract events, reminders, contacts, meeting links, verification codes from any screen. One tap to add to calendar, contacts, and more.</p></details>
