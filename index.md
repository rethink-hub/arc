<!-- FAQ -->
<div class="faq-section" id="faq">
  <div class="section-header">
    <span class="section-tag">FAQ</span>
    <h2>Frequently asked questions</h2>
  </div>
  <div class="faq-list">
    <details>
      <summary>Do I need to create an account?</summary>
      <p>No! You can use Arc as a guest. Google Sign-In is optional and only needed for cloud backup and community features.</p>
    </details>
    <details>
      <summary>Does Arc monitor my screen in the background?</summary>
      <p>Absolutely not. Arc only accesses screen content when you explicitly tap a button in the sidebar.</p>
    </details>
    <details>
      <summary>Are screenshots automatically captured?</summary>
      <p>No. Screenshots require your explicit permission and are only taken when you initiate an action that needs them.</p>
    </details>
    <details>
      <summary>Which apps work with Arc?</summary>
      <p>Arc works with virtually any app. However, it automatically disables itself in nearly 400 sensitive apps (banking, crypto, password managers) by default.</p>
    </details>
    <details>
      <summary>Is my data private?</summary>
      <p>Yes. Screen content is processed for AI responses but never stored on our servers. Your saved content stays on your device unless you choose Google Drive backup.</p>
    </details>
    <details>
      <summary>What are Custom Actions?</summary>
      <p>Custom Actions let you create your own AI commands. Write a prompt and save it to your sidebar for one-tap access.</p>
    </details>
    <details>
      <summary>What are Community Actions?</summary>
      <p>Community Actions are AI actions shared by other Arc users. Browse, search, and add them to your sidebar with one tap.</p>
    </details>
    <details>
      <summary>How does Chat with AI work?</summary>
      <p>Chat lets you have conversations about content on your screen. Ask questions, get clarifications, or explore topics further.</p>
    </details>
  </div>
</div>
